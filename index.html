Workflow
From zero to research-ready
in three steps.
01
Explore a Medical Problem
Browse the Problem Explorer. Pick any disease — click to see the full ML pipeline, real datasets with direct links, benchmark metrics, and why false negatives literally kill people in that domain.
→
02
Generate a Methodology
Describe your problem, pick your data type and skill level. Get a full AI-generated research roadmap: best model, datasets, evaluation metrics, and tips tailored to your experience level.
→
03
Build a Blueprint
Turn your idea into a complete science fair or hackathon plan — research question, hypothesis, 8-week timeline, success metrics. Save it and share it with a single click.
